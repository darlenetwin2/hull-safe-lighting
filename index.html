Why it matters

Traditional lighting installs can involve drilling, sealing, and hoping nothing shifts over time. Brian’s approach focuses on lighting that prioritizes hull protection and a clean installation.

Reduce leak risk

Fewer penetrations means fewer potential failure points over the life of the boat.

Protect gelcoat + resale

Cleaner installs help prevent cracking, corrosion points, and “what did the previous owner do?” energy.

Better light, smarter placement

Purpose-built layouts for docking, accent, and visibility. Bright where you want it.

FAQ

Is this truly no-drill?

The goal is to avoid drilling through the hull whenever possible. Some boats or layouts may require exceptions. We confirm your setup before you buy so you know what to expect.

Saltwater safe?

Components are selected for harsh marine environments, including moisture and corrosion exposure. Rinse and basic upkeep still apply, because boats.

Can I install it myself?

Many customers do. If you’d rather have it installed, we can recommend a path based on your boat and comfort level.

What boats does it fit?

Most setups are adaptable. Send your boat make, model, and a couple photos, and we’ll recommend the right kit and layout.
